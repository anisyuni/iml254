My Interest

Enjoying listening to good music is my interest lately, especially KPOP. I love KPOP boy groups especially NCT DREAM, TREASURE & RIIZE.

THIS IS NCT DREAM NCT DREAM is a 7 member boy group under SM Entertainment. The members are Lee Mark, Huang Renjun, Lee Jeno, Lee Haechan, Na Jaemin, Zhong Chenle & Park Jisung. Lee Mark was born in 1999 which makes him the oldest in the team. Huang Renjun, Lee Jeno, Lee Haechan & Na Jaemin are same age members, were born in 2000. Zhong Chenle was born in 2001. Lastly, Park Jisung was born in 2002 which makes him the youngest in the team.
THIS IS TREASURE TREASURE is a group formed through the survival show YG Treasure Box. The group consists of Choi Hyunsuk, Park Jihoon, Yoshi, Kim Junkyu, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Kim Doyoung, Haruto, Park Jeongwoo & So Junghwan. Choi Hyunsuk who is the oldest was born in 1999, Park Jihoon, Yoshi & Kim Junkyu were born in 2000, Yoon Jaehyuk & Asahi were born in 2001, Kim Doyoung was born in 2003, Haruto & Park Jeongwoo were born in 2004 & the youngest which is So Junghwan was born in 2005.
THIS IS RIIZE RIIZE is a 7 member boy group under SM Entertainment. The members are Shotaro, Song Eunseok, Jung Sungchan, Park Wonbin, Hong Seunghan, Lee Sohee & Lee Anton. They made their debut on September 4, 2023 with their single album, ’Get A Guitar’. Shotaro is the oldest member was born in 2000, Song Eunseok & Jung Sungchan were born in 2001, Park Wonbin was born in 2002, Hong Seunghan & Lee Sohee were born in 2003 & lastly, Lee Anton, the youngest, was born in 2004.
